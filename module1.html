1. What is Digital Marketing?

Digital Marketing is the promotion of products or services using online channels. Unlike traditional marketing, digital marketing allows precise targeting, tracking results, and engagement with the audience.

  • Channels include: Websites, SEO, Social Media, Email, Paid Ads, Content Marketing.
  • Benefits: Measurable results, cost-effective campaigns, audience targeting, global reach.

2. Traditional vs Digital Marketing

Traditional Marketing Digital Marketing
TV, Radio, Print Ads, Billboards Social Media, SEO, PPC, Email Marketing, Content Marketing
Hard to measure ROI Trackable & Measurable
One-way communication Interactive & Engaging
High cost Cost-effective

Digital marketing allows businesses to reach global audiences and adjust campaigns in real-time based on data.

3. Career Opportunities in Digital Marketing

  • SEO Specialist: Optimize websites to rank on Google.
  • Social Media Manager: Handle Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn marketing campaigns.
  • Content Marketer / Copywriter: Create blogs, videos, and ad copy.
  • Digital Marketing Analyst: Analyze data, traffic, and ROI.
  • PPC/Ads Manager: Run paid campaigns on Google, Meta Ads, etc.

4. How the Internet & Search Engines Work

Understanding the internet and search engines is essential for digital marketers:

  • Websites are hosted on servers that store files and data.
  • Search engines like Google crawl pages, index them, and rank them based on relevance.
  • SEO helps your website rank higher for specific keywords.
  • Example: If you search "Digital Marketing tips", Google shows pages it considers most relevant and trustworthy.
